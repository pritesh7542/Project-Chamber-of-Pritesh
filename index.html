About

Expertise in Tax Law,
A Heart for People

Pritesh Kumar is an advocate with extensive experience in direct and indirect taxation, alongside deep expertise in corporate and financial crime matters. He has handled complex cases across income tax, GST, customs, service tax, central excise, VAT, money laundering, and arbitration.

He specialises in GST advisory, income tax planning, and financial advisory, adeptly managing a broad spectrum of issues including white-collar and financial crimes. He regularly appears before various Tribunals and the Hon'ble Bombay High Court.

Beyond the courtroom, Pritesh is a dedicated advocate for the underprivileged — believing firmly that quality legal representation must be accessible to all. When not navigating the labyrinth of tax codes, he can be found on the cricket pitch.
